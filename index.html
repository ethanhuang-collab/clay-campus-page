Program Structure

A two-phase journey from learner to leader

Move from hands-on technical training to real strategic impact — all within a single program.

Phase 1

Alpha Forge

Guaranteed admission to Alpha Forge, Clay's 4-week, application-only GTME intensive. Traditionally reserved for industry professionals, this program provides hands-on experience with Clay and the modern GTM technology stack, including tools like Claude Code.

  • Gain a deep understanding of Clay and modern GTM workflows
  • Learn how today's leading growth teams operate
  • Receive Alpha Forge Certification
Graduates are added to Clay's internal GTME talent network with direct-to-interview access for exclusive roles.
Phase 2

Thought Partnership

Collaborate directly with Clay's team to co-design and launch the campus ambassador program. This is a 0→1 opportunity to help build a program from the ground up.

  • Contributing 5–10 hours per week leading up to the school year
  • Shaping the strategy and direction of the program
  • Creating digital content and assets
  • Representing Clay at student events and communities
  • Identifying growth opportunities on campus
  • Supporting event planning and execution
